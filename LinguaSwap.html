How LinguaSwap Works

Set Language Pair

Choose your native language and the language you want to learn. LinguaSwap will do the rest.

Add Words to Your List

Highlight words in your native language, right-click, and add them to your personalized vocabulary list.

Dynamic Replacement

As you browse, LinguaSwap will automatically replace the words you're learning with their translations.

Passive Learning

Exposure to the target language words through natural reading helps you remember them better.
